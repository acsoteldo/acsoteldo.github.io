Exhibiting at Texas Water 2022

If you are a previous Texas Water exhibitor and have accumulated points, we'll be reaching out to you in the upcoming weeks regarding exhibit sales for Texas Water 2022.

If you are new to Texas Water, it is always a sold out show with a waitlist for exhibitors (to be added to the waitlist, please send an email to exhibits@txwater.org). Due to this, we use a points system for exhibitors. Earning points could provide earlier access to the online booth purchasing system for 2023 and beyond.

Click here for a detailed explanation of the Texas Water points system. You can also earn points for advertising in the WEAT and TAWWA publications, the Texas Water Conference Preview and Program or through sponsorship of Texas Water.
